Climate News Website User Guide

1. Getting Started

To access the Climate News Website, follow these steps:

  • Open your web browser (e.g., Chrome, Firefox, Safari).
  • Enter the URL of our website in the address bar and press Enter.
  • You will be directed to the homepage of our website.

2. Viewing News Articles

On the homepage, you will find a collection of recent climate news articles. Each article card typically includes the title, publication source, publication date, thumbnail image, and a link to read more.

Navigation

  • Scrolling: You can scroll down the homepage to view more news articles.
  • Pagination: If there are multiple pages of articles, you can navigate between them using pagination links at the bottom of the page.

3. Reading Articles

To read a specific article, follow these steps:

  1. Click on the title or "Read more" link of the article you're interested in.
  2. You will be redirected to the full article on the source website in a new tab.
  3. Read the article at your leisure.
  4. Close the tab when you're done reading to return to the Climate News Website.

4. Additional Features

Our website may include additional features to enhance your experience. Some common features include:

  • Search Functionality: Look for a search bar where you can enter keywords to find articles on specific topics.
  • Categories or Tags: Explore articles categorized by topics or tagged with keywords for easier navigation.
  • Subscription Options: Consider subscribing to our newsletter or notifications to stay updated on the latest news.

Support and Feedback

If you encounter any issues while using our website or have suggestions for improvement, please don't hesitate to contact us. Your feedback is valuable in helping us enhance your browsing experience.

Thank you for using the Climate News Website!
